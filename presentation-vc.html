The Problem

A 2,000-Year Musical Tradition Is Fading Away

Indian classical music represents one of humanity's most sophisticated art forms -- yet it faces an existential crisis. Knowledge is concentrated in aging masters, access is limited to cultural insiders, and younger generations are disengaging.

  • Raga knowledge passed orally through guru-shishya tradition is at risk
  • Global audiences cannot access or appreciate ragas in familiar formats
  • No technology platform preserves ragas with full music theory
  • Classical music audience is shrinking while global demand for Indian culture grows

The Solution

AI-Powered Cultural Preservation Through Music Generation

Raga Radio encodes complete raga theory into software and uses AI to generate authentic compositions in modern genres -- making ancient music accessible to everyone.

Digital Raga Archive

68+ ragas encoded with aaroha (ascending), avaroha (descending), vadi (king note), samvadi (queen note), pakad (signature phrase), thaat, time, mood, and season. The most comprehensive digital raga database ever built.

AI Composition Engine

GPT-4 generates musically authentic melodies following Alap-Jor-Jhala structure with ornamentations. Each melody strictly follows raga grammar -- ascending/descending rules, note emphasis, and signature phrases.

Multi-Genre Fusion

8 genre profiles transform ragas into Classical, Atmospheric, Metal, Electronic, Lo-fi, Jazz Fusion, World Fusion, and Orchestral styles -- creating 544+ unique raga-genre combinations.

Full Production Pipeline

AI melody to MIDI (midi-writer-js) to WAV synthesis (44.1kHz sine wave) to Cloudflare R2 upload to Suno API production. Professional-quality tracks from theory to mastered audio.

Competitive Advantage

Deep Moats in Unique IP

Raga Radio's defensibility comes from proprietary musicological data, specialized AI pipelines, and first-mover advantage in an untouched niche.

Proprietary Raga Database

68+ ragas with complete music theory -- aaroha, avaroha, vadi, samvadi, pakad, thaat, time, mood. No other platform has this data in machine-readable format.

Specialized AI Pipeline

Custom GPT-4 prompts that enforce raga grammar rules. The AI understands ascending/descending sequences, note hierarchy, and Alap-Jor-Jhala structure.

Swara-Western Bridge

Proprietary converter mapping 12 Indian swaras (with komal/tivra variants) to Western notation and MIDI. Enables seamless cross-cultural music generation.

Multi-Genre Engine

8 genre profiles with mood mapping, BPM ranges, instrument sets, and production styles. Any raga in any genre -- 544+ combinations no competitor offers.

Cross-Platform Presence

Live web app + iOS SwiftUI wrapper ready for App Store. First-mover in AI raga music on mobile, building user habits and library lock-in.

Cultural Authority

Building the definitive brand for AI-powered Indian classical music. Community and content moat deepens with every track generated.
